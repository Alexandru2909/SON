Abstract

This page contains the following: How to use the app

Steps

  1. Signup/Login: To start using the app you need to create an account, open the /signup page and enter your credentials. If you already own an account, you will be redirected to the the /login page automatically.
  2. Link your accounts: Enter the /links page and let us access your data by toggling on the checkboxes and entering your usenames from the following sites:
    • LastFM
    • Linkedin
    • Twitter
    • VK
    You will only have to click 'Give rights' buttons.
  3. Friends: Now you can see the friends from the logged social networks and check their profiles out. You can search for their names, sort them by location or by the social network you know them from.
  4. Acquaintances: If some of your friends from other social networks have accounts on our page, they will appear on the /acquaintances page and you can thus complete your social network by befriending them.
  5. Graph: After logging in, you can check out the graph generated by you and your connections on the /graph page based on the social network selected. This graph can be exported as FOAF file format.
  6. Extension: Before logging in, press the Sync button on the extension panel to let the extension know who you are. From now on, whenever you enter any of the social network sites, you will get a notification with the number of suggested friends ( acquaintances ). In the panel, select the social network you want to see your suggestions from and click the 'Get acquaintances' button to make them appear.
