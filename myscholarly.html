Application architecture

Goals

Our web solution provides a platform for social networking, this means our users will be able to collect profiles from social networking sites like: Instagram, Facebook, Github,.. The users' profiles can be searched by multiple criterias, ex: hobbies, geographical areas, name, etc.. Also, the network for each user will have the possibility to be exported in a 2D manner as PNG

4. Application Scenarios

  1. A new user makes an account, logs in and fills in his personal info and his own social networks profile links.
  2. To extend his network, he may recieve a sugestion regarding the possible friends in our app found in another social network.
  3. He may modify his own network by:
    • Adding friends
    • Deleting friends
  4. The logged user can find other users based on criterias such as name, location, etc..
  5. Receive notifications about pending requests for connections. And accept/decline them.
  6. Export his own network as .png file.
